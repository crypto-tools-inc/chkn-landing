Features

Token Visualisation

Generates detailed, real-time flowgraphs that map Solana token transactions, providing intuitive insights into liquidity patterns and market dynamics.

Advance Flow Analysis

Leverages AI-powered scanning to identify suspicious trading patterns, market manipulations, and unusual token distribution strategies across DEXes and CEXes.

Visual Transaction Tracking

Offers color-coded, interactive graphs displaying transaction types, wallet balances, transfer volumes, and key holder movements.

Market Risk Detection

Provides specialized scans tracking token flows and launches to help users quickly detect potential trading anomalies and market risks.

Commands

  • /s <ticker or token address>

    Generate a flowmap of current token balances and their transfers based on the last 6 hours of transfer data.

  • /sa <ticker or token address>

    Same as /s but includes AI-powered analysis of the token flows.

  • /first <ticker or token address>

    Generate a flowmap of the token balances and transfers after the first 5k transfers.

  • /fa <ticker or token address>

    Same as `/first` but includes AI-powered analysis of the token flows.

  • /holders <token address>

    Generate a list of the top holders of a token.

  • /legend

    A textual guide to interpreting CHKN flowmaps.

  • /subscribe

    Purchase a CHKN Computer subscription.
